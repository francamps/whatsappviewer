WhatsApp Conversation Explorer

How to use this

To use this dashboard, you'll have to use your own whatsapp app to email the conversation you'd like to explore to yourself.

In order to do that, enter the conversation and on the top right, tap on the settings icon (usually the three-dot design) corner. Tap on 'More', and then 'Email conversation'. Write your own email. Shortly after, you will receive an email with a *.txt attached.

Open that file and paste in the following form to start.

And don't worry, your conversation won't even hit the server. Everything happens in the frontend, so nothing to worry about privacy.
